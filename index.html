Executive Proposal

This proposal outlines my plan to develop a professional, live website that will serve as an IT Supervisor Readiness Portfolio. The goal is to demonstrate my qualifications, leadership potential, and readiness for a supervisory or junior IT management role in the U.S. technology industry. This site will act as a centralized portfolio—showcasing essential project work, performance tools, leadership resources, and communication strategies aligned with modern IT leadership expectations.

Leadership Vision

My goal is to become a values-driven IT leader who promotes operational excellence, inclusive culture, and strategic innovation. I lead by example with integrity, empathy, and accountability—focusing on continuous learning, cross-team collaboration, and data-informed decision-making. This portfolio reflects these principles, presenting concrete evidence of my ability to lead projects, support teams, and drive performance outcomes.

Website Scope & Structure

  • Home Page – Professional headshot, leadership vision, and IT career goals.
  • Portfolio Tasks – Weekly project deliverables showcasing core competencies.
  • Gantt Chart – Project timeline with phases and deadlines.
  • Coaching & Communication Toolkit – Templates for employee development and feedback.
  • KPI & Budget Tools – Dashboards for performance tracking and resource planning.
  • Customer Service Playbook – Workflows for service management and support efficiency.
  • Ethics & Culture Plan – Guidelines supporting team ethics, inclusion, and cultural readiness.
  • Contact Page & Résumé – Downloadable résumé and professional contact form.
