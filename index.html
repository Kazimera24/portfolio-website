2. Causal Loop Diagram: Supervisor Clarity and Performance Loop

This diagram illustrates how clear communication and team productivity reinforce each other in a positive feedback loop.

Loop Name: Supervisor Clarity and Performance Loop

Variables:
  • Supervisor Feedback Clarity
  • Team Understanding of Goals
  • Task Accuracy
  • Productivity
  • Supervisor Trust in Team
Loop Explanation:

When a supervisor provides clear and consistent feedback, the team better understands expectations.

This leads to fewer mistakes and more accurate task execution, which improves overall productivity.

Higher productivity builds trust between the supervisor and team, leading to even more open communication—creating a reinforcing feedback loop.
